About This Vehicle

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz GLE 450 4MATIC represents the perfect balance of luxury, performance, and versatility. This mid-size luxury SUV delivers an exceptional driving experience with cutting-edge technology and refined comfort.

Powered by a 3.0L inline-6 turbocharged engine with EQ Boost mild-hybrid technology, the GLE 450 produces 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. The 48-volt system provides seamless power delivery and improved fuel efficiency.

Inside, the cabin features the stunning dual 12.3-inch displays of the MBUX infotainment system, premium leather upholstery, and ambient lighting with 64 colors. The E-ACTIVE BODY CONTROL suspension delivers unparalleled ride comfort.

This example is finished in Selenite Grey Metallic with Macchiato Beige/Espresso Brown interior. Equipped with the Premium Package, Driver Assistance Package, and 21-inch AMG wheels.
